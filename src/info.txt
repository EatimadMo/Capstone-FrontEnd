The idea of ​​the project is to create a platform that brings together volunteers and the disabled, through which they can communicate and provide various services such as education, escort, transportation, etc.
 Initial contact is by phone.
 The website asks the user to log in, either as a volunteer or a disabled person, and asks them to enter more additional information required. and upon completion of registration,
On the home page, when you press the buttons, a list of all the information and services required or granted will be displayed, along with job opportunities.
